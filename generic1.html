Employee Attrition - HR Analytics

The data source is from IBM HR dataset on Kaggle website. This project mainly focuses on expressing our logic and procedures of using machine learning algorithms to predict employee attrition. We performed data pre-processing and use under sampling method to balance our datsset. After that, we built five models to predict employee turnover, which are Logistic Regression, Neural Network, Decision Tree, Random Forest, XGboost.
The importance of features and the performance of each model shown as below.

We took recall and accuracy as our evaluation metrics. Since in our scenario, false negative, the model predicts the employee will stay but s(he) actually leave, may cause more serious effects to the company. Among all the five models, random forest achieves the highest accuracy score (0.993) and highest recall (0.992). This indicates that random forest is able to make the most accurate prediction and has the lowest FN risk.
Therefore, in our project, random forest is the best model for employee churn prediction.
