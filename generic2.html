COVID-19 Data Visualization

The data source is from Johns Hopkins COVID-19 datasets.
Start from 2020, 22nd January to 4th June. Base on the data, we performed analysis on global and US coronavirus transmission and death rate.

(Click the graph to play around with the dashboard)

Trajectory graph is used with time series animation to understand the infection status of each country. For countries who take effective actions, they will gradually drop underneath the trend line, deviate from the exponential growth and show a downward trend. We made further analysis on the measures conducted by those countries who show successful signs of controlling the spread.

We applied similar analysis methods on US to show its situation on COVID-19. After that, we provided scenario estimation and recommendations (base on global experiences) to US officer to control the pandemic. We hope everyone in the globe can resume to normal life as soon as possible.
