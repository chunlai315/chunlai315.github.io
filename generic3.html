Telecom Customer Churn Prediction

This is an individual project. I used R to build classification models to analyze the likelihood of customer churn and the important factors that cause customers to unsubscribe. In the process, data visualization was applied to show the ratio of churn or stay among customers with different using habits.
During the data pre-processing, data was split in 70%-30% train and test set. Since the data is imbalanced, I chose to use under sampling strategy to balance the trainset. Two models, decision tree (CART) and logistic regression, were built in this project. After comparison, CART showed better performance than Logistic Regression, no matter in accuracy, sensitivity, or specificity.

Based on the model, all the features in the data were evaluated to identify which are important factors to affect customersâ€™ decision. With the knowledge of important features, we can make suggestions to telecom company to adjust the price plan or offer promotion to specific users to improve the customer retention
